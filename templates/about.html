About The Vayu

Bilaspur's premier destination for wellness, beauty, and relaxation since 2020

Our Story

Founded in 2020, The Vayu Unisex Salon & Spa was born from a vision to create a sanctuary of wellness and beauty in the heart of Bilaspur. Our name "Vayu" symbolizes the flow of life energy that we aim to restore in every client who walks through our doors.

What started as a small spa with just 3 therapists has grown into Bilaspur's most trusted wellness destination, serving over 500 satisfied clients. We believe in the transformative power of touch, aromatherapy, and personalized care.

Our commitment to excellence has made us the go-to destination for professional massage therapy, beauty treatments, and spa services in Chhattisgarh.
